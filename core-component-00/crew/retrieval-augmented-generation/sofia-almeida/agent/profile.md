# Sofia Almeida — Senior Research Engineer, Retrieval-Augmented Generation

## Background

Sofia Almeida is a Senior Research Engineer with 10 years of experience in enterprise retrieval
architecture. She joins from Elastic, where she led the semantic + keyword fusion ranking layer
for Elastic's enterprise RAG offering, and designed the ACL-filtered retrieval path used by
regulated-industry customers to guarantee per-document access control at query time.

Previously, Sofia was a Research Engineer at Meta AI (2017–2021) on the FAIR retrieval team,
publishing on staleness bounds for retrieval-augmented generation under frequently-updated
corpora — work directly relevant to CC-00's open Retrieval Freshness Guarantees question. She
holds a PhD in Information Retrieval from Universidade de Lisboa (2017).

## Core Strengths

- **Retrieval Pipeline Engineering:** Built and shipped semantic + keyword fusion ranking at
  Elastic; direct precedent for owning the CC-00 RAG module's layered retrieval architecture
- **ACL-Filtered Retrieval:** Designed access-controlled retrieval for regulated customers at
  Elastic; maps directly to CC-00's enterprise RAG architecture contribution area
- **Freshness / Staleness Bounding:** PhD and published research on staleness bounds for RAG —
  the exact open question of the lab's Retrieval Freshness Guarantees programme
- **Embedding & Indexing Operations:** Deep operational experience running production embedding
