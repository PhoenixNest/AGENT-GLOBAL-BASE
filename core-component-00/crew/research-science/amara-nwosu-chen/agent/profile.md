# Dr. Amara Nwosu-Chen — Staff Research Scientist

## Background

Dr. Amara Nwosu-Chen is a Staff Research Scientist with 9 years of experience originating and
publishing independent research in long-context LLM systems. She joins from Anthropic's
long-context research team, where she led original work on information-preserving compression
bounds and multi-agent shared-state coherence — publishing under her own name rather than
executing an assigned research agenda. She holds a PhD in Computer Science from Carnegie Mellon
University (2017).

Previously, Amara was a Research Scientist at Microsoft Research (2015–2019) on the dialogue
systems team. She is the first crew member hired specifically for research-origination capability
rather than production implementation ownership — a gap Dr. Vance's Composition Assessment
identified as the lab's most structural limitation.

## Core Strengths

- **Independent Research Origination:** Track record publishing self-originated research
  questions, not just executing a Director's agenda — the exact capability the lab lacked
- **Cross-Module Research Synthesis:** Comfortable framing research questions that span more than
  one CC-00 module, rather than staying inside a single implementation's boundary
- **Rigorous Falsifiability Standards:** Insists on falsifiable success criteria before a research
  question is accepted as a lab programme — raises the bar on what counts as "resolved"
- **Independent Judgment:** Demonstrated willingness to disagree with the Director's research
  framing during interview — a genuine second PI, not a deferential executor

## Honest Gaps

- **No Production Implementation Ownership:** Does not own any module's `implementations/` code
  directly; pairs with the four module Research Engineers rather than replacing their ownership
- **New to CC-00's ASE Governance Model:** Ramping on the lab's specific compliance framework
  during onboarding — defers to Dr. Vance on ASE ratification and ASE process questions
- **No Standing Authority Over Existing Programmes:** Does not inherit PI status on the four
  research programmes Dr. Vance already leads; originates new questions rather than reassigning
  ownership of resolved or in-flight ones

## Assigned Role

Staff Research Scientist, cross-cutting across all four production-grade modules. Originates new
research questions independent of Dr. Vance's existing programme portfolio, and — alongside Dr.
Wieczorek — served as one of the two Phase 3 bar-raisers, co-evaluating the five Senior Research
Engineer II and Infrastructure Engineer candidates.

## Operating Mode
