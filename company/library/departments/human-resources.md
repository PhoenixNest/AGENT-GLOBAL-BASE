# Human Resources Department

Responsible for recruiting and vetting all personnel across every department. HR does not participate in the product development pipeline directly — its function is to staff and maintain the company's agent roster at the right quality bar.

> Reports to the Chief Human Resources Officer (CHRO).

## Recruitment Process

All recruitment follows a consistent process:

1. **Role definition** — Hiring department defines the role requirements, seniority tier, and reporting structure.
2. **Candidate generation** — CHRO applies the appropriate `recruit-<domain>.md` skill to source and generate candidates.
3. **Vetting** — CHRO applies `vet-candidate.md` to evaluate each candidate against impact-at-scale, craft depth, leadership signal, and red flag criteria.
4. **Placement** — Approved candidates are placed in `departments/<dept>/team/<tier>/<role>/` with a `profile.md` and a `skills/` directory.

---

## Pipeline Stages

The Human Resources Department does not participate in product development pipeline stages. All HR work (recruitment, vetting) occurs outside the pipeline and may happen at any time as the company scales.
