# Legal Department

The company's dedicated legal function, providing counsel across corporate governance, technology transactions, commercial agreements, regulatory compliance, and cross-border data privacy. The CLO oversees all legal matters for both company and studio operations and is a direct C-suite report to the User.

> Established: 2026-05-12. Triggered by the absence of legal guidance during the SMS-001 subway monitoring project, which exposed the company's inability to accurately assess cross-border data-handling obligations for sensitive national and municipal datasets.

## Pipeline Stages

| Stage                                             | Role                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        |
| ------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Stage 1** — Requirements → PRD + SRD            | **CLO:** Reviews PRD for legal feasibility; confirms no prohibited use cases; identifies jurisdiction-specific compliance requirements that must be incorporated into the SRD. Associate Counsel produces initial DPIA screening for any personal-data processing in scope. |
| **Stage 3** — Prototype → UML Engineering Package | **CLO:** Reviews data architecture against cross-border data transfer obligations; confirms that the proposed storage jurisdiction satisfies any applicable localisation requirements; flags any data-element that triggers government-sector regulations.                  |
| **Stage 6** — Architecture & Conformance Review   | **CLO:** Signs off on legal conformance — confirming data handling, cross-border transfers, and data architecture comply with all applicable law in the target jurisdictions.                                                                                               |
| **Stage 8** — Integrity Verification              | **CLO:** Legal clearance for launch — confirms all legal obligations satisfied, no outstanding compliance gaps, data subject rights mechanisms operational.                                                                                                                 |
| **Stage 10** — Release Readiness Check            | **CLO:** Final legal sign-off before launch. Confirms no post-Stage-8 changes have introduced new legal risk.                                                                                                                                                               |
