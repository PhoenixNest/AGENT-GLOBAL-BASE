# Product Management FY2026 Q2 Recruitment Plan

**Plan Version:** 2.0
**Headcount:** 2 FTEs — VP Product, Web Platforms · VP Product, API & Developer Platforms
**Hiring Model:** Parallel (both funnels run simultaneously)
**Recruitment Window:** ~4 weeks (extensible to 8)
**Onboarding:** 90 days per VP (Immerse 30 → Contribute 30 → Lead 30)
**Status:** Approved
**Plan Owner:** CPO (Marcus Tran-Yoshida) + CHRO (Dr. Evelyn Hartwell)

---

## What This Plan Is

The Product Management division is adding two VP-tier leaders to close a structural gap: the CPO is the named Stage 1 PRD author for four pipelines but has native craft in only one (Mobile). This plan hires a Web Product VP and an API Product VP, each reporting to the CPO, each taking primary Stage 1 authorship for their pipeline after a 90-day integration.

The plan is designed as a standalone Product Management initiative, sized for the current 4-pipeline scale and the 3-person Product Management division that results.
