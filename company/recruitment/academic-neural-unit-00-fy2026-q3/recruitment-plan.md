## Executive Summary

> Following the ANU-00 formation meeting on 2026-07-23
> (`academic-neural-unit-00/formation/2026-07-23-formation-meeting/`), Dr. Vance's advisory group
> recommended a phased build-out (2 hires now, 3 flagged for later). The CEO reviewed that
> recommendation and overrode the phasing: **full-scale recruitment, all 5 identified roles hired
> in a single cohort**, so that ANU-00 launches as a fully-resourced, comprehensive academic
> research organization from day one rather than growing into one incrementally. The CEO further
> authorized CHRO to open and execute this cycle immediately under the standard pipeline, with
> results reported only upon completion — no intermediate check-in required. This plan documents
> that single-cohort cycle.

> **Amendment (2026-07-23, v1.1) — Phase 2: Elite Expansion Cohort.** Following the CEO's approval
> of Dr. Mokoena's stage-of-inquiry charter refinement
> (`academic-neural-unit-00/formation/2026-07-23-formation-meeting/formation-report.md` §3.1) and
> a subsequent directive to intensify research in LLMs, machine learning, AI, and agent
> engineering, the CEO told Dr. Mokoena not to be frugal with headcount and to recruit to the
> standard of top Ivy League universities or leading research institutions, then **delegated full
> execution authority for this expansion to Dr. Mokoena** — the same delegation pattern the CEO
> used for Dr. Vance during CC-00's own Phase 3
> (`company/recruitment/core-component-00-fy2026-q3/recruitment-plan.md` v1.3). As with that
> precedent, delegated execution does not bypass CHRO's pipeline authority: Dr. Mokoena runs this
> phase's sourcing and technical evaluation, and CHRO's office still signs every candidate through
> the 9-stage pipeline and holds final hiring authority. Per Dr. Mokoena's own stated position
> (recorded in this cycle's advisory discussion), "Ivy League / leading-institution standard" is
> operationalized as **evidentiary caliber** — publication record and impact at the level a top
> institution's own faculty search committee would demand — not an institutional-pedigree
> screening criterion, consistent with CHRO's existing evidence-over-brand vetting philosophy and
> the pipeline's own Elite Talent Bar. Phase 2 adds 5 roles; see § Phase 2 below.

### Span-of-Control Check (required before this plan leaves Draft status)

**Phase 1:** max span was 4 (ANU-00 Lead's direct reports), within the healthy 5–8 range.

Research Scientist (Foundational AI Lead) absorbs 2 of the 4 new L3s (LLM Systems, Applied AI

within the healthy 5–8 / small-team range.

consistent with the CEO's no-direct-link ruling
(`academic-neural-unit-00/formation/2026-07-23-formation-meeting/formation-report.md` §2).

## Phased Hiring Timeline

Per the CEO's full-scale directive, this is **one hiring phase**, not a multi-cycle build-out. The
sequencing below is an internal execution order within that single phase — standard bar-raiser
staggering, not a deferral of any role to a future quarter.

### Phase 1: Full Founding Cohort (Weeks 1–6) — CEO-approved 2026-07-23, full-scale

| Seq | Role                                           | Level | Vetting Authority                             | Tier Floor |
| --- | ---------------------------------------------- | ----- | --------------------------------------------- | ---------- |
| 1.1 | ANU-00 Lead                                    | L4    | CHRO + Dr. Vance (technical/incubation input) | 18/20      |
| 1.2 | Research Scientist — Generalist                | L3    | CHRO + ANU-00 Lead                            | 17/20      |
| 1.3 | Research Scientist — AI / Neural Networks      | L3    | CHRO + ANU-00 Lead                            | 17/20      |
| 1.4 | Research Scientist — Software Engineering / CS | L3    | CHRO + ANU-00 Lead                            | 17/20      |
| 1.5 | Knowledge Systems Engineer                     | L3    | CHRO + ANU-00 Lead                            | 17/20      |

**Note on Dr. Vance's role (1.1 only):** consistent with `core-component-00/crew/README.md`'s
Authority Scope ("None of the CC-00 crew recruit or evaluate personnel — that is CHRO authority"),
Dr. Vance's participation on the Lead hire is **technical/incubation input only**, not a
co-decision-making vetting authority — the hiring decision itself rests with CHRO. Once the Lead
is hired (1.1), Dr. Vance's involvement in this cycle ends; 1.2–1.5 are evaluated by CHRO + the new
ANU-00 Lead only.

**Phase gate:** All 5 roles filled with candidates meeting or exceeding tier floor; ANU-00 crew
folder structure established at `academic-neural-unit-00/crew/` and all 5 agent profiles
published; Dr. Vance's incubation involvement formally ends.

### Phase 2: Elite Expansion Cohort (Weeks 1–6) — Mokoena-directed, CEO-delegated execution, 2026-07-23

| Seq | Role                                            | Level | Vetting Authority                             | Tier Floor             |
| --- | ----------------------------------------------- | ----- | --------------------------------------------- | ---------------------- |
| 2.1 | Staff Research Scientist — Foundational AI Lead | L4    | CHRO + ANU-00 Lead                            | 18/20 + Leadership ≥ 4 |
| 2.2 | Research Scientist — Machine Learning Theory    | L3    | CHRO + ANU-00 Lead                            | 17/20                  |
| 2.3 | Research Scientist — LLM Systems                | L3    | CHRO + ANU-00 Lead + Staff Research Scientist | 17/20                  |
| 2.4 | Research Scientist — Applied AI Systems         | L3    | CHRO + ANU-00 Lead + Staff Research Scientist | 17/20                  |
| 2.5 | Research Scientist — Agent Systems Research     | L3    | CHRO + ANU-00 Lead + Staff Research Scientist | 17/20                  |

**Sequencing rationale (Dr. Mokoena's own, carried into execution unchanged):** 2.1 hires first as
bar-raiser, exactly mirroring 1.1's role in Phase 1. 2.2 (Machine Learning Theory) follows next,
since it extends existing coverage (Dr. Baek's neural-network specialty) rather than opening new
territory. 2.3–2.4 (LLM Systems, Applied AI Systems) fill next, co-evaluated by the new Staff
Research Scientist once seated. 2.5 (Agent Systems Research) is sequenced last, deliberately — the
role most likely to be misread as encroaching on CC-00's `multi-agent-engineering/` mandate, so the
other three Phase 2 hires' chartered output exists first to make the boundary self-evident by
precedent rather than by charter language alone.

**Phase gate:** All 5 Phase 2 roles filled with candidates meeting or exceeding tier floor,
screened to the evidentiary-caliber standard defined in the Amendment above; reporting lines
restructured per the Span-of-Control Check (Staff Research Scientist absorbs 2 direct reports);
all 5 agent profiles published under `academic-neural-unit-00/crew/research-science/`.

## Compensation Strategy

Compensation follows the standard L3/L4 bands defined in the CHRO-owned quarterly configuration
artifact `compensation-bands.md` (`company/pipeline/recruitment/pipeline.md` § Quarterly
Configuration Cycle). No role-specific adjustments were requested for this cycle.

**No-outsourcing policy.** Per the CEO's "dedicated research organization" directive, all 5 roles
are budgeted and compensated as direct FTEs under standard `compensation-bands.md` levels. No
portion of this cycle's scope may be fulfilled through a contractor, consultancy, or
outsourced-research arrangement. If a role cannot be filled as a direct FTE through standard
pipeline channels, it is escalated to CHRO for re-scoping, not routed around this policy.
