# Commercial Contracts

| Competency               | Description                                                                  | Quality Criteria                                                                           |
| ------------------------ | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| Contract Review          | Review inbound and outbound agreements for legal risk                        | All material risk positions identified; redlines substantiated with legal rationale        |

## Execution Guidance

### Contract Review Red Flags

Flag the following for CLO review regardless of contract value:

| Red Flag                                         | Why It Matters                                                          |
| ------------------------------------------------ | ----------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Unlimited liability clause                       | Company exposure uncapped; not acceptable without board approval        |
| Broad IP assignment from company to counterparty | Assigns ownership of our technology to the counterparty                 |
| Data sharing without DPA                         | GDPR / PDPA violation if personal data is shared without a DPA in place |
| Auto-renewal with no notice                      | Creates binding obligation without intentional renewal decision         |
| Governing law = high-risk jurisdiction           | Litigation in an unfamiliar or adverse jurisdiction                     |
| Change-of-control trigger                        | Can terminate or accelerate the agreement on M&A activity               |

### IP Ownership Matrix Template

For each agreement, complete:

| IP Category      | Owner                  | Licensed To | Restrictions                      |
| ---------------- | ---------------------- | ----------- | --------------------------------- |
| Background IP    | Originating party      | Other party | Non-exclusive, non-transferable   |
| Developed IP     | [Agree in negotiation] | —           | As defined                        |
| Client Data IP   | Client                 | Company     | Processing only; no sub-licensing |
| Derivative Works | [Agree in negotiation] | —           | As defined                        |
