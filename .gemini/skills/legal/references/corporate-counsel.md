# Corporate Counsel

| Competency         | Description                                                         | Quality Criteria                                                                          |
| ------------------ | ------------------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Entity Management  | Maintain corporate records, registrations, and annual filings       | All entities in good standing; no missed filings; cap table accurate                      |
| Employment Matters | Review employment agreements, equity grants, and HR legal questions | Standard employment agreements used; equity grants conform to approved plan terms         |
| Legal Operations   | Manage legal intake, prioritisation, and matter tracking            | All legal requests logged within 1 business day; priorities set by risk × urgency         |
| Contract Lifecycle | Own the contract repository and renewal tracking for all agreements | Contract repository current; renewals flagged 60 days in advance; no auto-renewals missed |

## Execution Guidance

### Legal Intake Triage

When a legal request arrives, assess on two axes:

| Axis    | Questions                                                                 |
| ------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Risk    | Could this expose the company to liability? What is the maximum downside? |
| Urgency | Does this block a pipeline stage, a deal, or a customer commitment?       |

Escalate to CLO immediately if:

- Matter involves potential litigation or enforcement action
- Counterparty is a government body
- Exposure exceeds $250K
- Matter involves personal data of >10,000 individuals

### Employment Agreement Review Checklist

- [ ] IP assignment clause present and broad enough to cover all work product
- [ ] Non-solicitation scope appropriate for jurisdiction
- [ ] Confidentiality obligations survive termination
- [ ] At-will (US) or statutory notice period (other jurisdictions) correctly stated
- [ ] Equity grant terms reference the approved equity plan
- [ ] Governing law clause matches the employee's work location
