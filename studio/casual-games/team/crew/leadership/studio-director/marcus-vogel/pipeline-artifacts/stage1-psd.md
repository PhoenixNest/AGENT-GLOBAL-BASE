## Success Criteria

### 90-Day Milestones

- [ ] Studio vision document drafted and approved by CTO/CPO/CDO panel
- [ ] Hiring plan for Phase 2 (Creative + Production) finalized and launched
- [ ] Pipeline standards document authored for all 11 stages
- [ ] First team retrospectives conducted with Phase 1 leadership cohort
- [ ] Studio culture principles defined and published

### 6-Month Milestones

- [ ] Phase 2 and 3A hiring completed (22 total FTEs onboarded)
- [ ] Stage 0 (Art Direction) and Stage 1 (Concept) completed
- [ ] Vertical Slice (Stage 3) production underway with clear quality bar
- [ ] Studio operational cadence established (sprints, reviews, playtests)
- [ ] First revenue projection model validated with CPO

### 12-Month Milestones

- [ ] Full Production (Stage 5) on track with Gantt schedule adherence ≥90%
- [ ] Soft Launch (Stage 8) readiness confirmed with KPI targets met
- [ ] Team retention rate ≥90% across all divisions
- [ ] Studio P&L within 10% of budget forecast
- [ ] Post-launch content roadmap defined through first 6 months of Live Ops

## Notes

- This role is the **single point of accountability** for the game studio — they own the vertical slice, full production, and soft launch
- The Studio Director must demonstrate ability to lead cross-disciplinary teams (engineering, art, design, audio, production, live ops)
- Prior experience with **casual/F2P mobile games** is strongly preferred given the studio's target market
- The candidate must be comfortable reporting to both the User (CEO) and CTO (Dr. Kenji Nakamura) for strategic oversight
