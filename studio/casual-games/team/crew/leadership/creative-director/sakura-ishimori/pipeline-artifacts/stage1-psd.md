## Success Criteria

### 90-Day Milestones

- [ ] Art direction document (Stage 0) completed with mood boards and visual pillars
- [ ] GDD co-authored with Lead Game Designer (Stage 1)
- [ ] Creative hiring criteria defined for Phase 2 (Art Director, Lead Game Designer)
- [ ] First art review cadence established with art team candidates
- [ ] Player-first design philosophy documented and socialized with studio

### 6-Month Milestones

- [ ] Prototype (Stage 2) creative elements complete and validated
- [ ] Vertical Slice (Stage 3) creative quality bar defined and met
- [ ] Art Director and Lead Game Designer onboarded and ramping
- [ ] Complete visual style guide for all game assets
- [ ] First playtest conducted with creative quality assessment

### 12-Month Milestones

- [ ] Full Production (Stage 5) creative content pipeline producing at target velocity
- [ ] Art team fully staffed (7 FTEs) and operating at creative quality standard
- [ ] Soft Launch (Stage 8) creative elements validated with player testing
- [ ] D1 retention creative contribution measurable and meeting targets
- [ ] Creative vision documented and transferable (Lead Game Designer can carry vision)

## Notes

- This role owns **Stage 0 (Art Direction)** and **Stage 1 (Concept)** creative vision — they define the visual pillars that the entire studio builds on
- The Creative Director must demonstrate mastery of both art direction AND game design — this is a hybrid creative leadership role
- **Player-first design philosophy** is non-negotiable — the studio rejects predatory monetization patterns
- IAP-only monetization expertise is preferred (no ads-based revenue models)
- The candidate must be comfortable working with the Studio Director and reporting to them while maintaining creative independence
