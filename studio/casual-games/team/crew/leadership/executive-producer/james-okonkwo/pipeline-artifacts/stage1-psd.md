## Success Criteria

### 90-Day Milestones

- [ ] Production planning framework (Stage 4) drafted and approved by Studio Director
- [ ] Phase 2 production hiring criteria defined (Producer, Associate Producer)
- [ ] Risk register template created and populated with Phase 1 risks
- [ ] Sprint cadence established with Studio Director and Creative Director
- [ ] Budget model for Phase 2–4 hiring validated with CPO

### 6-Month Milestones

- [ ] Stage 4 (Production Planning) completed with approved Gantt and asset list
- [ ] Producer and Associate Producer onboarded and ramping
- [ ] Risk mitigation plans active for top 5 identified risks
- [ ] Cross-functional sprint velocity tracking operational
- [ ] Stage 7 (Soft Launch Prep) production plan drafted

### 12-Month Milestones

- [ ] Full Production (Stage 5) on schedule with ≥90% milestone adherence
- [ ] Stage 9 (Global Launch Readiness) checklist complete
- [ ] Budget variance within 10% of forecast
- [ ] Team velocity stable across all divisions
- [ ] Post-launch content production pipeline operational

---

## Notes

- This role owns **Stage 4 (Production Planning)** and **Stage 9 (Global Launch Readiness)** — they translate creative vision into executable production plans
- The Executive Producer must demonstrate ability to manage $10M+ budgets and coordinate 30+ person cross-functional teams
- Experience with **F2P mobile game launches** is strongly preferred
- The candidate must be comfortable reporting to the Studio Director while maintaining production independence
- Risk management expertise is critical — this role owns the risk register for the entire 11-stage pipeline
