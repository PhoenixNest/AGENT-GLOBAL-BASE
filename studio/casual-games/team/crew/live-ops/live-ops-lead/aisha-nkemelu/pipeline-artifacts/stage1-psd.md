## Role Summary

The Live Ops Lead owns the post-launch content strategy, community engagement, KPI analysis, event design, and virtual economy management for the casual games portfolio. This role is the primary driver of player retention and monetization after launch, operating across pipeline Stages 7, 8, and 10. Reports to the Executive Producer.

## Minimum Qualifications

- **7+ years** of live ops experience in mobile gaming (F2P required)
- Proven track record of managing content roadmaps for games with **1M+ DAU**
- Demonstrated expertise in **A/B testing frameworks** and statistical analysis
- Experience designing and executing **seasonal events** with measurable KPI impact
- Deep understanding of **free-to-play economy design** and monetization loops
- Strong community management experience across Discord, Reddit, and social platforms
- Proficiency with analytics tools (Firebase, Amplitude, GameAnalytics, or equivalent)
