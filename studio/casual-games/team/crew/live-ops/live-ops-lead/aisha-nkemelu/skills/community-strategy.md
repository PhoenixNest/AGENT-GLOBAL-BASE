# Community Strategy

## Overview

This skill covers the design, execution, and measurement of community engagement strategies for F2P mobile games. It encompasses Discord community management, social media strategy, player feedback integration, and community-driven content design.

## Tools & Frameworks

| Tool/Framework           | Purpose                                | Proficiency Level |
| ------------------------ | -------------------------------------- | ----------------- |
| Discord                  | Community platform management          | Expert            |
| Sprout Social            | Social media scheduling and analytics  | Advanced          |
| Reddit                   | Community engagement and AMAs          | Advanced          |
| Typeform / Google Forms  | Player surveys and feedback collection | Advanced          |
| Sentiment analysis tools | Community mood tracking                | Intermediate      |
| In-game feedback system  | Direct player-to-dev communication     | Advanced          |

## Core Methodologies

### 1. Community Growth Framework

| Phase      | Duration   | Focus                                        | Key Metric                              |
| ---------- | ---------- | -------------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------- |
| Foundation | Months 1–2 | Platform setup, moderation, content cadence  | Community size, engagement rate         |
| Growth     | Months 3–6 | Events, partnerships, user-generated content | DAU in community, content creation rate |
| Maturity   | Months 6+  | Self-sustaining community, player leadership | Organic growth, sentiment score         |

### 2. Player Feedback Loop

```
Player Feedback → Categorize (bug, feature, balance, UX) → Prioritize → Action → Communicate Back → Measure Impact
```

**Response SLA:**

- Bug reports: Acknowledge within 24 hours, update within 72 hours
- Feature requests: Acknowledge within 48 hours, add to roadmap review
- Balance concerns: Acknowledge within 24 hours, data review within 1 week
- General feedback: Acknowledge within 48 hours

### 3. Community-Driven Event Design

- **Community Vote Events:** Let players vote on next event theme or reward (drives engagement + ownership)
- **Creator Spotlights:** Feature community-created content (fan art, strategy guides, videos)
- **AMA Cadence:** Monthly AMA with dev team (rotating: Live Ops, Design, Engineering)
- **Beta Tester Program:** Invite top community members to test events before launch

## Scenario: Community Crisis Management

**Situation:** A poorly balanced event causes player backlash — Discord sentiment turns negative, Reddit threads critical, social media mentions spike.

**Approach:**

1. **Acknowledge (within 2 hours):** Public statement acknowledging the issue
2. **Investigate (within 24 hours):** Data analysis to understand scope of impact
3. **Act (within 48 hours):** Deploy fix or compensation
4. **Communicate (within 72 hours):** Detailed post-mortem shared with community
5. **Prevent:** Implement review process for future events to catch balance issues pre-launch

**Trade-offs:**

- Fast response may mean incomplete information; slow response risks escalation
- Compensation sets precedent; must balance player satisfaction with economy health
- Transparency builds trust but requires admitting mistakes publicly

## Quality Standards

- Community sentiment score monitored daily (target: ≥ 75% positive)
- Response SLA met for 95%+ of player feedback
- Monthly community health report published internally
- Discord growth rate ≥ 10% MoM during first 6 months
- At least 1 community-driven event per quarter
