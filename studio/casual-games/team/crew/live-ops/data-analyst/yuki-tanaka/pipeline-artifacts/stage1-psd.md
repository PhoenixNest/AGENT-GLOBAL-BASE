## Role Summary

The Data Analyst owns game analytics, KPI dashboard creation, A/B test design, cohort modeling, LTV forecasting, and player segmentation analysis for the casual games portfolio. Reports to the Live Ops Lead. Operates across pipeline Stages 5, 8, 9, and 10.

## Minimum Qualifications

- **3+ years** of game analytics or related data analysis experience
- Demonstrated expertise in **cohort modeling** (cohort decay modeling, retention curve analysis)
- Experience with **LTV forecasting** models (parametric, ML-based, or hybrid)
- Strong **SQL** proficiency and **Python** data analysis skills (pandas, numpy, scikit-learn)
- Experience building and maintaining **KPI dashboards** (Tableau, Looker, or equivalent)
- Understanding of **A/B test design** and statistical inference
- Familiarity with game analytics platforms (Firebase, Amplitude, GameAnalytics)
