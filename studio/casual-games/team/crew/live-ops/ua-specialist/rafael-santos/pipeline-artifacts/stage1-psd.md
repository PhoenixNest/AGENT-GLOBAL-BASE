## Role Summary

The UA Specialist owns user acquisition strategy and execution for the casual games portfolio, managing paid media campaigns across Meta Ads, Google UAC, and Apple Search Ads. Responsible for CPI optimization, ROAS tracking, and App Store Optimization (ASO). Reports to the Live Ops Lead. Operates across pipeline Stages 8, 9, and 10.

## Minimum Qualifications

- **3+ years** of mobile game user acquisition experience
- Proven track record managing $50K+/month ad spend across Meta Ads, Google UAC, and Apple Search Ads
- Demonstrated expertise in **CPI optimization** (achieved CPI below $2.00 for casual games)
- Experience with **ROAS tracking** and attribution platforms (AppsFlyer, Adjust, Branch)
- Strong understanding of **ASO** best practices for iOS and Android
- Proficiency with analytics tools and data-driven decision making
- Creative strategy experience: ad concept development, creative testing frameworks
