### Item 7: Diversity and Inclusion Considerations

**Verdict: ⚠️ CONDITIONAL PASS**

**What I found:** The recruitment plan contains **zero references** to diversity, inclusion, EEO, or demographic considerations. There is no D&I strategy, no diversity sourcing targets, and no demographic tracking in the pipeline artifacts.

**What the roster shows organically:** The hired cohort is notably diverse by name/origin indicators:

- **Geographic diversity:** Names suggest representation from European (German, French, Polish, Russian, Italian, Spanish, Swedish), African (Nigerian, Kenyan), Asian (Japanese, Chinese, Indian, Korean), Middle Eastern, and Latin American backgrounds.
- **Gender diversity:** Mix of male and female candidates across all levels and divisions.
- **No single demographic dominance** in any division.

**However:** This appears to be **organic diversity through merit-based sourcing**, not the result of a deliberate D&I strategy. There is no documented commitment to inclusive hiring practices, no bias mitigation in the automated pipeline, and no demographic data collection for compliance or improvement tracking.

**Recommendation:** Implement a lightweight D&I framework before the next hiring cycle (e.g., 3-game scaling). This should include: (1) demographic data collection (voluntary, anonymized), (2) bias audit of automated screening algorithms, (3) diverse sourcing channel targets, and (4) inclusive interview panel composition requirements.
