**Owner:** CHRO Office (Dr. Evelyn Hartwell)  
**Status:** Active

**Trigger:** Stage 0 Art Direction Gate  
**Audit Reference:** CHRO Audit Concern 4 — "Phase 1 Artifact Backfill"

## 1. Executive Summary

The CHRO audit identified that **Phase 1 candidates** — the first three hires in the casual games studio — had their pipeline artifacts **backfilled after initial recruitment**. These candidates were recruited and onboarded before the full artifact pipeline was operational. Their pipeline documentation was created retroactively to maintain audit completeness.

This document flags all backfilled artifacts for **mandatory quality review** during the Stage 0 Art Direction gate. The review ensures that backfilled artifacts meet the same quality standards as artifacts produced during live recruitment.

### Candidates Affected

| G-ID | Name            | Role               | Division   | Artifacts Backfilled |
| ---- | --------------- | ------------------ | ---------- | -------------------- |
| G1   | Marcus Vogel    | Studio Director    | Production | 8 (Stages 1–4, 6–9)  |
| G2   | Sakura Ishimori | Creative Director  | Design     | 8 (Stages 1–4, 6–9)  |
| G3   | James Okonkwo   | Executive Producer | Production | 8 (Stages 1–4, 6–9)  |

**Total: 24 backfilled artifacts across 3 candidates.**

### Why This Matters

Backfilled artifacts are created retrospectively — after the candidate has already been hired and is performing in their role. This introduces two risks:

1. **Content accuracy risk:** Retrospective documentation may not accurately reflect what was actually assessed during recruitment. There is a risk of post-hoc rationalization — writing artifacts that look correct rather than documenting what was actually observed.
2. **Consistency risk:** Backfilled artifacts were not produced under the same conditions, timelines, or review processes as live artifacts. They may not match the formatting, depth, or rigor of subsequently produced artifacts.

Neither risk implies that the candidates are unqualified. All three Phase 1 hires have demonstrated strong performance in their roles. The purpose of this flag is **quality assurance of documentation**, not re-evaluation of hiring decisions.

## 3. Quality Review Process for Stage 0 Gate

Before Stage 1 recruitment begins, the Stage 0 Art Direction gate must include a **backfilled artifact quality review**. This review is conducted by the following panel members:

### 3.1 CDO Review — Content Quality & Consistency

**Reviewer:** Yuki Tanaka-Chen, CDO  
**Scope:** All backfilled artifacts (24 total across 3 candidates)

**Review Criteria:**

| Criterion                                                    | Pass Threshold                      |
| ------------------------------------------------------------ | ----------------------------------- |
| Content depth matches live artifacts                         | ≥ 90% of live artifact depth        |
| Formatting consistent with pipeline standards                | 100% compliance                     |
| Claims are supported by evidence in the artifact             | Zero unsupported claims             |
| Consistency with Stage 2 deliverables (design org artifacts) | All design-relevant claims verified |

**Output:** CDO quality review sign-off or remediation list.

### 3.2 CTO Review — Technical Accuracy

**Reviewer:** Dr. Kenji Nakamura, CTO  
**Scope:** Backfilled artifacts for G1 (Studio Director) and G3 (Executive Producer) — 16 artifacts

**Review Criteria:**

| Criterion                                                  | Pass Threshold                      |
| ---------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------- |
| Technical claims are accurate and defensible               | 100% accuracy                       |
| Technical assessments referenced are valid                 | Zero invalid or outdated references |
| Engineering competencies described match role requirements | 100% alignment                      |

**Output:** CTO technical accuracy sign-off or remediation list.

### 3.3 CSO Review — Security Compliance

**Reviewer:** Dr. Sarah Chen, CSO  
**Scope:** All backfilled artifacts with security-relevant content (24 total)

**Review Criteria:**

| Criterion                                              | Pass Threshold          |
| ------------------------------------------------------ | ----------------------- |
| Security competencies described match SRD requirements | 100% alignment          |
| Security clearance documentation is complete           | Zero missing items      |
| No security-relevant claims are unsupported            | Zero unsupported claims |

**Output:** CSO security compliance sign-off or remediation list.

### 3.4 Remediation

Any quality gaps identified during the Stage 0 gate review must be **remediated before Stage 1 entry**. Remediation may include:

- Rewriting artifacts to meet quality standards
- Adding missing evidence or references
- Correcting inaccurate claims
- Updating formatting to match pipeline standards

Remediated artifacts must be versioned (e.g., `artifact-v1-backfill.md` → `artifact-v2-remediated.md`) and the original must be preserved for audit trail.
