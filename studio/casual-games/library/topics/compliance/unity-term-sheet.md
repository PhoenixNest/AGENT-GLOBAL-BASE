## 3. Key Clauses

### 3.1 Termination & Wind-Down

| Provision                       | Detail                                                                                                                                               |
| ------------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Termination for Convenience** | Either party may terminate with 180 days written notice                                                                                              |
| **Termination for Cause**       | Either party may terminate immediately upon material breach if uncured after 30 days written notice                                                  |
| **Wind-Down Period**            | **12 months** from termination effective date for all published games to remain operational and distributed                                          |
| **Post-Wind-Down**              | After wind-down period, Licensee must either (a) migrate to alternative engine, or (b) negotiate separate runtime license for continued distribution |
| **Refund on Early Termination** | Pro-rata refund of unused prepaid seat license fees if terminated for Unity's material breach                                                        |

> **Risk Mitigation:** The 12-month wind-down period eliminates the "immediate kill switch" risk identified during the CIO C1 legal review. Published titles retain distribution rights, protecting user base and revenue continuity.

### 3.2 Audit Rights

| Provision              | Detail                                                                                                                                                       |
| ---------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Audit Frequency**    | Annual, upon 30 days written notice                                                                                                                          |
| **Audit Scope**        | Seat license utilization, revenue reporting for Runtime Fee applicability                                                                                    |
| **Audit Process**      | Unity-designated independent auditor; Licensee cooperation required                                                                                          |
| **Cure Period**        | **30 days** from audit findings to remedy any discrepancy                                                                                                    |
| **Audit Cost**         | **Unity bears all audit costs** unless a discrepancy exceeding **5%** of reported figures is identified, in which case Licensee bears reasonable audit costs |
| **Dispute Resolution** | Binding arbitration under AAA Commercial Rules, San Francisco venue                                                                                          |

> **Risk Mitigation:** The 5% discrepancy threshold and Unity-borne audit cost for minor discrepancies protect against punitive audit practices. The 30-day cure period provides adequate remediation time.

### 3.4 Data Privacy & Compliance

| Provision                     | Detail                                                                                                                                     |
| ----------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **GDPR Compliance**           | Unity processes all personal data in accordance with EU General Data Protection Regulation (Regulation (EU) 2016/679)                      |
| **CCPA Compliance**           | Unity complies with California Consumer Privacy Act as amended by CPRA                                                                     |
| **COPPA Compliance**          | Unity provides **child-directed game compliance tooling** for all titles targeting or knowingly collecting data from children under 13     |
| **Data Processing Agreement** | Incorporated by reference; Unity acts as Data Processor for Licensee's Data Controller obligations                                         |

> **Risk Mitigation:** Explicit COPPA and FERPA provisions address the student/child-directed game risk identified during legal review. The 24-hour breach notification exceeds the standard 72-hour requirement, enabling faster incident response.
