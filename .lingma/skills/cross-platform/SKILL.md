# Cross-Platform

## Overview

Cross-platform mobile development using Kotlin Multiplatform (KMP) and Flutter. Owned by the Cross-Platform Development Lead (Mei-Ling Johansson). KMP is used for shared business logic between Android and iOS; Flutter is used for standalone cross-platform applications.
