# Architecture

## Overview

Software architecture and technology strategy for the mobile product company. Owned by the CTO (Dr. Kenji Nakamura), CIO (Dr. Priya Mehta), and the Software Architect (Rafael Okonkwo). All architecture decisions are documented as Architecture Decision Records (ADRs) and technology selections are captured in the Technology Selection Document (TSD).
