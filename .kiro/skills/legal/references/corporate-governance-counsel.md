# Corporate Governance Counsel

| Competency            | Description                                                            | Quality Criteria                                                                                  |
| --------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Board Support         | Draft resolutions, advise on fiduciary duties, and prepare board packs | All resolutions accurately reflect the decision; fiduciary advice cites applicable jurisdiction   |
| Governance Frameworks | Design and maintain corporate governance policies and procedures       | Policies are jurisdiction-appropriate, approved by CLO, and integrated into the operations manual |
| Legal Risk Register   | Maintain a quarterly legal risk register across all legal risk domains | Register covers all active projects; each risk has a severity, owner, and mitigation action       |
| Entity Management     | Manage corporate entity structures, registrations, and annual filings  | All entities in good standing; deadlines tracked 60 days in advance                               |

## Execution Guidance

### Legal Risk Register Structure

```
Legal Risk Register
├── Technology & IP Risks
│   ├── Open-source licence exposure
│   ├── Third-party IP infringement claims
│   └── Platform policy compliance (App Store, Google Play)
├── Data & Privacy Risks
│   ├── Cross-border data transfer compliance gaps
│   ├── Data breach exposure
│   └── DPIA outstanding items
├── Contractual Risks
│   ├── Evergreen contracts without renewal review
│   ├── Indemnity exposure in vendor agreements
│   └── Change-of-control triggers
└── Regulatory Risks
    ├── New legislation monitoring
    ├── Licence expiry tracking
    └── Government investigation exposure
```

### Governance Review Trigger Points

| Trigger                         | Action Required                                                 |
| ------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------- |
| New product launch              | Board resolution; update governance documentation               |
| New market entry                | Entity registration assessment; local counsel engagement        |
| Material contract ≥ $500K       | CLO sign-off; board notification if ≥ $2M                       |
| New data processing activity    | Legal risk register update; DPIA scoping with Associate Counsel |
| Pipeline Stage 1 (PRD approval) | Legal feasibility memo; compliance requirements documented      |
| Pipeline Stage 10 (release)     | Final governance clearance; legal sign-off in release checklist |
