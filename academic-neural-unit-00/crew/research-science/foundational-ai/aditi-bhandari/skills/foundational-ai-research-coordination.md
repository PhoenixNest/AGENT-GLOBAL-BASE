# Skill: Foundational AI Research Coordination

**Owner:** Dr. Aditi Bhandari, Staff Research Scientist — Foundational AI Lead
**Purpose:** Coordinate research across the LLM Systems and Applied AI Systems specialties she
leads, applying the stage-of-inquiry charter test (`formation-report.md` §3.1) at the coordination
layer between Dr. Mokoena and her 2 direct reports.

---

## When to Use

Whenever Dr. Dubois (LLM Systems) or Dr. Tan (Applied AI Systems) proposes a new research question,
before it goes to Dr. Mokoena for chartering
(`academic-neural-unit-00/crew/lead/naledi-mokoena/skills/research-programme-chartering.md`).

## Process

1. **Apply the stage-of-inquiry test first.** Before anything else, check whether the proposed
   question is pre-implementation ("does this work / is it worth building" — ANU-00's charter) or
   post-validation ("given that it works, how do we build it reliably" — CC-00's charter). This is
   the primary per-programme test per the CEO-approved refinement; the specific field (LLM, AI,
   agent engineering) is not the deciding factor.
2. **Resolve overlap between the two specialties before escalating.** LLM Systems and Applied AI
   Systems were split from a single broader role once headcount pressure lifted — some questions
   will still sit at the boundary. Decide primary ownership based on whether the question is
   LLM-specific (Dubois) or cross-technique (Tan) before either researcher invests significant time.
3. **Pass upward for chartering, not downward for execution.** This role coordinates and
   pre-screens; it does not unilaterally approve a research programme — that ratification stays
   with Dr. Mokoena per her chartering skill, even for questions originating under this lead.
4. **Track cumulative coverage.** Periodically check that LLM Systems and Applied AI Systems
   coverage isn't quietly drifting back toward duplicating each other, given they share a recent
   common origin as one role.

## What This Skill Does Not Cover

- Research-question origination or evidencing rigor itself — that belongs to Dr. Dubois's or Dr.
  Tan's own eventual skill development in their specialties.
- Final chartering authority — that remains Dr. Mokoena's alone.
