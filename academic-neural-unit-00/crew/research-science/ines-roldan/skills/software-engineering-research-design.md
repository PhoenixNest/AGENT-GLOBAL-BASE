# Skill: Software Engineering Research Design

**Owner:** Dr. Inés Roldán, Research Scientist (Software Engineering / Computer Science)
**Purpose:** Design falsifiable software-engineering and core-CS research questions for ANU-00's
knowledge base, explicitly scoped apart from CC-00's production-engineering mandate.

---

## When to Use

Any chartered research programme (per `research-programme-chartering.md`) whose primary domain is
software engineering methodology or core computer science, where the output is a research finding
about how software engineering works — not a shippable engineering module.

## Process

1. **State the falsifiable hypothesis and its unit of measurement.** Empirical software
   engineering claims (e.g., productivity, technical-debt accumulation) require an explicit metric
   and population before they can be evaluated — a vague claim like "technical debt slows teams
   down" is not chartered until it specifies what is measured and over what population.
2. **Boundary-check against CC-00 explicitly, every time.** Before starting, state in one sentence
   why this question is academic research (a finding about software engineering as a discipline)
   and not production engineering (a pattern or module CC-00 would own). If the distinction isn't
   obvious, escalate to Dr. Mokoena before proceeding rather than guessing — this is the specific
   discipline this candidate demonstrated unprompted at vetting; institutionalize it here.
3. **Source from empirical literature first.** Check for existing empirical studies addressing the
   same or an adjacent question before designing new data collection — replication and extension
   are valid, valued outcomes, not lesser ones.
4. **Report effect sizes, not just significance.** Empirical software-engineering findings must
   report magnitude and confidence, not only a binary significant/not-significant result.
5. **File the knowledge-base entry** under the dated `YYYY-MM-DD-<slug>/research-report.md`
   convention.

## What This Skill Does Not Cover

- Neural-network or AI-systems research questions — route to Dr. Baek
  (`skills/neural-systems-research-design.md`).
- Building any tooling described in a finding as a reusable pattern — that would be a
  production-engineering deliverable, out of ANU-00's charter entirely; note it as a referral, not
  as ANU-00 output.
