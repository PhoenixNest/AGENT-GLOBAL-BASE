# Skill: Neural Systems Research Design

**Owner:** Dr. Yuna Baek, Research Scientist (AI / Neural Networks)
**Purpose:** Design falsifiable neural-network and AI-systems research questions and evidence
findings to publication-grade rigor for ANU-00's knowledge base.

---

## When to Use

Any chartered research programme (per `research-programme-chartering.md`) whose primary domain is
neural network theory, architecture design, or applied AI systems research.

## Process

1. **State the falsifiable hypothesis.** Every programme in this domain must have a stated
   condition under which the hypothesis would be considered disproven — not just a direction of
   inquiry. This is the same discipline flagged in vetting as this candidate's core strength; hold
   every programme to it, not only the ones easy to falsify.
2. **Ground in existing literature before original work.** Check whether the question has already
   been answered or partially answered in published literature — replicate or extend existing
   findings before assuming original investigation is needed. Independent replication is valued
   here as highly as novel results.
3. **Separate theoretical claims from empirical claims.** A generalization-bound argument and an
   empirical architecture-search result require different evidence standards — state which kind of
   claim is being made before presenting supporting evidence.
4. **Translate for the knowledge base's non-specialist audience.** Per the Honest Gaps noted in
   this role's own vetting record, every finding must include a plain-language summary
   accessible to a reader outside neural-network theory, in addition to the full technical
   treatment — do not publish technical-only.
5. **File the knowledge-base entry** under the dated `YYYY-MM-DD-<slug>/research-report.md`
   convention.

## What This Skill Does Not Cover

- Software-engineering-specific research questions — route to Dr. Roldán
  (`skills/software-engineering-research-design.md`).
- Cross-field synthesis spanning neural networks and another charter field — route to Dr.
  Ibarra-Costa (`skills/cross-domain-literature-synthesis.md`) with this skill's findings as input.
