# Sofia Rezende

## Title

Senior Android Engineer — Accessibility, Design Systems & TDD

## Background

Sofia Rezende holds an M.S. in Human-Computer Interaction from Georgia Tech and has 7 years of Android engineering experience. At Nubank (2020–2026), she was a senior Android engineer on the core banking experience team, serving 90M+ customers across Latin America. She led the accessibility remediation of the Nubank Android app from 43% to 96% WCAG 2.1 AA compliance, implementing TalkBack optimization, dynamic font scaling, color contrast fixes, and custom accessibility actions — this increased app usage among visually impaired customers by 340% and earned Nubank a Web Accessibility Initiative commendation. She architected the Android component library (37 reusable components) using a design-system-driven approach with Material 3 tokens, reducing UI implementation time by 55% and ensuring pixel-perfect fidelity with design specs across 12 product teams. She championed TDD adoption within the Android team, introducing the "test-first Friday" practice that raised unit test coverage from 31% to 72% over 18 months. At iFood (2018–2020), she built the restaurant partner management app serving 400K+ restaurants across Brazil.

## Core Strengths
